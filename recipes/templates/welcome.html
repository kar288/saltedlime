Salted Lime

Keep track of recipes and notes

Add your favorite recipes to the app, rate, share and organize them!
  • Sign in with Google
  • Sign in with Facebook
You can add recipes to the application in three simple ways:
Import bookmarks at once
Import all your bookmarks from a browser. You can later on select links that you want to import.
Add a recipe with a link
Add a new recipe by copying the link directly from your favorite website. Add it and it will automatically be part of your recipes.
Add a recipe manually
Organize your favorite recipes by adding all the information manually.
Automatically imported recipes will contain all available information from the source like ingredients, instructions, categories, and servings.
  • Organize your recipes with tags.
  • Add notes to keep track of your changes to the recipe.
  • Share them with friends.
  • Search for recipes based on what you have in the kitchen.
  • Find recipes that contain seasonal ingredients.
